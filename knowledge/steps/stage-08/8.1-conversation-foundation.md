## Risks introduced
- Single `ai_worker` process: a long background summary/pool job in flight can still delay a chat turn
  (`at_front` only reorders the queue, not a running job). Mitigated by limiter interactive headroom;
  a dedicated `ai_interactive` worker is a future option (open question).
- History is packed into `ContextRefs.transcript_text` (the registry only templates `{{transcript}}`).
  Acceptable contract stretch; richer templating deferred.
