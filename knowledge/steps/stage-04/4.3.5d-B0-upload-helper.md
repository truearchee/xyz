## Contract mapped
- Upload endpoint: `POST /modules/{module_id}/sections/{section_id}/assets`.
- Replace endpoint: `PUT /modules/{module_id}/sections/{section_id}/assets/{asset_id}`.
- Multipart field: `file`, from `backend/app/api/routers/content.py`.
- Response DTO: generated `SectionAssetResponse`.
- Auth/session utility: existing `OpenAPI.TOKEN`, configured in `frontend/src/lib/api/wrapper.ts`, backed by the Supabase browser session and E2E forced-token override.
- Error behavior: `401` signs out and redirects to `/login`; `403` throws `ForbiddenError` and preserves the session; other non-2xx responses throw generated `ApiError`.
