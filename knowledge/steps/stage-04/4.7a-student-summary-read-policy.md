- **┬º4/┬º6 `summary.status == generated/failed` reconciled to the as-built model (code wins).**
  `GeneratedLectureSummary` has no `status` column (success-only) ÔÇö "generated" = an eligible row exists;
  "failed"/"generating" derive from the `overallState`/`steps` projection. The spec ┬º6 notes already
  mandate consulting `overallState`. Intent preserved; recorded in the spec ┬º6 "Mechanism reconciliation".
- A `/student/modules/{id}/sections` coarse-list endpoint was added (┬º8.1) returning a per-section
  `summaries_state` computed in batched queries (no per-section fan-out / no per-section projection ÔÇö a
  documented list-hint; the detail endpoint is authoritative).
