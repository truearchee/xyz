## Method (the load-bearing discipline)
- **className conversion, DOM/attributes byte-preserved.** On every E2E-dense surface, `style={}` →
  semantic-token `className`; the element, `data-testid`, `role`, `aria-live`, `aria-label`, and form
  labels are unchanged. Components (Button/Badge/Card/EmptyState) swapped in ONLY where they don't touch
  a hook (e.g. active/inactive → `Badge`); E2E-critical buttons kept as raw `<button>` so their exact
  `data-testid`/name/disabled survive.
- **Hex → semantic tokens** per the plan's mapping; the two solid status colors stay tonal for body text.
- **panelStyles (hold #1):** the shared admin CSS-object map became `panelClasses` (className strings,
  same shape). **Renamed deliberately** so the compiler flags any dangling importer — tsc is clean and a
  `panelStyles` grep is comment-only ⇒ **zero dangling importers**.
