## Summary
Session 4.2 is complete. A successful transcript upload now enqueues a parse job, conditionally moves the transcript from `uploaded` to `queued`, and the worker reads raw transcript bytes through `StorageProvider.get_object`.

The parse handler creates an idempotent `ingestion_jobs` row, parses VTT/TXT into immutable `transcript_segments`, leaves successful transcripts at `status='parsing'`, and records parse completion in the job ledger. Failure paths clear partial segments, mark the transcript/job failed, and store sanitized error messages that exclude storage keys, URLs, credentials, stack traces, raw transcript text, and cue payload content.

No endpoint, frontend UI, chunking, embedding, summary, retry/backoff, replacement, supersession, or AI behavior was added.

## Risks introduced
- If enqueue fails after upload commit, the transcript remains `uploaded` until the future 4.6 sweeper.
- If the worker crashes mid-parse, the transcript can remain `parsing` with a `running` job until 4.6 stale-running recovery.
- Speaker extraction is intentionally conservative and can under-attribute unusual formats.
