## Findings
- The first real-provider attempt reached K2Think but failed as `InvalidOutput(not_json)`. Redacted
  metadata showed `status_code=200`, `echo_match=True`, and `finish_reason='length'` at `max_tokens=900`.
  The output contained JSON markers but no complete schema-valid JSON object.
- Fix: raise `recommendation_copy/v1` `max_tokens` from `900` to `2000` and update `CHECKSUMS.json`.
  This mirrors the earlier K2-Think-v2 inline-reasoning behavior: the model can spend substantial tokens
  before emitting the final JSON even for short copy.
