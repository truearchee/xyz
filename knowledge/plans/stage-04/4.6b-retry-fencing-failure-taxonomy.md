## Approach
The retry endpoint is thin: authorize the lecturer for the section, target a transcript by id (active or
pending; superseded â†’ 409), resolve the earliest failed step over the DAG, reset those jobs to `queued`,
enqueue after commit, return the projection. The heavy lifting (delete-and-regenerate) stays in the
workers and is fenced: before any destructive write, re-read job + transcript FOR UPDATE and abort if
superseded or no-longer-running. Summaries are decoupled to fork from parse so an embed failure can't
block them (the matrix). Each step records a sanitized failure_category; the projection rolls it up to
one of 9 categories + a `retryable` flag.
