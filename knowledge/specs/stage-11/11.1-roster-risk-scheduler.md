## Goal
Ship the Stage 11.1 foundation: a shared scheduler component, recorded `AgentRun`s, deterministic per-course
risk classification, persisted risk snapshots, lecturer roster risk visibility, and the student gentle view.

## Why now
Stage 11 cannot build proactive recommendations, deadline checks, or workload planning until a scheduler exists.
11.1 also locks the canonical deterministic risk contract used by the rest of Stage 11. AI is explicitly out of
scope; this session computes and displays deterministic data only.

## Build
- Add `AgentRun` and `StudentRiskSnapshot` tables in migration `0056`, chained after `0041`.
- Add a Python scheduler service/container under `platform/scheduler`; it is timezone-aware, singleton-locked,
  and only enqueues recorded `AgentRun` jobs onto existing RQ workers.
- Add an admin-only manual trigger endpoint that records `triggeredByUserId`, rate-limits/idempotently reuses the
  same run, and computes deterministic snapshots only.
- Add deterministic live-on-read per-course risk classification using `algorithmVersion="risk-v1"`, config-backed
  thresholds, `inputHash`, and `sourceCutoffAt`.
- Persist scheduled/manual snapshots with bounded history retention.
- Add lecturer roster risk APIs/UI: tier, reasons, cited metrics, sort/filter, and `Needs support: N`.
- Add student gentle risk APIs/UI: reason `studentText` and next steps only; no tier label, no peer data.
- Add ADR-056 for scheduler/risk decisions.

## Do not build
- No 11.2 recommendations, lecturer drafts, student nudges, AI phrasing, AI prompts, AIRequestLog writes, or
  real-provider smoke.
- No 11.3 assessment analysis, topic mastery analytics, or small-cohort insight UI.
- No 11.4 workload planner, availability settings, 6-phase planning algorithm, or plan records.
- No 11.5 `.ics` export.
- No 11.6 grade-forecast advice.
- No assistant or gamification domain edits, and no dependency on Stage 10 data.
- No new `StudentActivityEvent` types.

## Data model changes
- `agent_runs`: trigger/scope, scheduled time, triggering admin, algorithm version, status/timestamps, counts,
  idempotency key, failure message.
- `student_risk_snapshots`: run, student, module, `riskTier`, structured `riskReasons`, algorithm/input/source
  reproducibility fields, computed time.

## API changes
- `POST /admin/analytics/agent-runs`
- `GET /admin/analytics/agent-runs/{run_id}`
- `GET /lecturer/modules/{module_id}/analytics/roster-risk`
- `GET /student/modules/{module_id}/risk`

## Worker / job changes
- New RQ enqueue helper and worker job for `AgentRun`.
- New scheduler process/container that creates or reuses due `AgentRun`s and enqueues them.

## Authz rules
- Admin may manually trigger and poll `AgentRun`s.
- Lecturer may read risk only for modules where they are an active lecturer member.
- Student may read only their own module risk.
- Manual trigger endpoint returns 403 for lecturer/student roles.
